Privacy Policy

Personal Information

Personal information refers to data that can identify an individual, either directly or indirectly. For a home services platform, this typically includes information provided by both customers and service providers during registration or service booking.

Customer Information: Name, email, phone number, address, payment details, service preferences. Service Provider Information: Name, business name, contact details, certifications, experience, banking information (for payments).

Purpose of Collection: Personal information is collected to: Facilitate the matching of service providers with customers. Enable booking, payment, and scheduling of services. Provide customer support and handle feedback or complaints.

Protection of Information and Storage

Data Protection: Protecting user data is crucial for building trust. The platform implements various security measures to safeguard personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or destruction.
